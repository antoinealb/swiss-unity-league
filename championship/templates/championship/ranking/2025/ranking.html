Every year, Magic: The Gathering tournaments in Switzerland award SUL Points to players. At the end of the season (31.10.2025), the top 36 players with the most SUL Points qualify for the two-day SUL Championship tournament. Additionally, top finishers in the most prestigious tournaments of the season will qualify directly for the SUL Championship. The best players will also receive a bye (a free win) for the SUL Invitational.
