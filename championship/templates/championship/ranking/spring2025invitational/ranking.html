The SUL Invitational Spring is a one-day invite-only tournament on 13th of April 2025 where the top 4 players will receive a direct invite to the SUL Championship later this year.
Info about Spring Invitational

Only events from 01.11.2024 to 31.03.2025 are considered for this leaderboard. The following players qualify for the Spring Invitational:

  • The top 80 players with the most League Points
  • The top 2 players of each Premier event
  • The winner of each Regional (with top 4/8)
