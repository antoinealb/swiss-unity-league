In 2023, participating Magic: The Gathering tournaments in Switzerland awarded SUL Points to players. At the end of the season (31.10.2023), the top 40 players with the most SUL Points qualified for the two-day SUL Invitational tournament. The best players also received byes, which allowed them free wins in the initial rounds of the SUL Invitational.
