Every year, participating Magic: The Gathering tournaments in Switzerland award SUL Points to players. At the end of the season (31.10.2024), the best 40 players will be qualified for the two-day SUL Invitational tournament. The top players will also receive a bye (a free win) for the SUL Invitational.
