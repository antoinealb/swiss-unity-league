Here are some things to keep in mind when uploading results.

  1. Make sure to always upload the standings of the last swiss round (without points for top 8).
  2. Ensure players' names are entered as first name followed by last name separated by a space. Example: John Doe.
    • When using pairing tools like Aetherhub where the players can enter their names themselves, please remind players to input their real names accordingly.
  3. When using Eventlink make sure to submit tournament results right after the tournament. On the following day player names can be replaced with [[Redacted]].
  4. For events with Playoffs:
    1. First, upload the standings of the last Swiss round as usual and click submit.
    2. Next, you will be redirected to the view of the event where you see the uploaded results.
    3. Click on the button called 'Add/Edit Playoff Results'.
    4. Enter the results of the top 4 or top 8.
