Please register on eventfrog.ch/leoninmodern to buy your ticket for this real life event in Zürich, Switzerland! Aetherhub is only used for pairings/results!

In the box "Name" please enter your real firstname and lastname with a space in between. (Keep in mind that after sending the name, it will show your username again. However, your first- and lastname will be sent correctly.)

You don't have to add a decklist during registration, but please do so before the first round of the tournament (Sunday 9.10. 09:45). Any time before that, you can also change the decklist. The last uploaded decklist is used for the tournament. Make sure your decks visibility is set to private, otherwise everyone can see your deck. You can create a deck under: DeckHub -> MyDecks

Your registration is definite when you payed your entry fee on Eventfrog. The fact that AetherHub says your entry is not approved yet has no meaning. ("waiting for Tournament Organizer to approve your entry."). You can check that you completed all the steps on this page (please allow for a 15 minute delay).

Only judges and streamers have access to this account and can see decklists.

In case you have questions, please reach out to dbdmtg@gmail.com

Terminology

OMW Opponents Match Win Percentage

GW Your Game win Percentage

OGW Opponents Game Win Percentage

The final standings are determined by the total points of participants. For tiebreakers you first look at OMW% then GW% and last OGW%. If there is an exceptionally rare reason and all of these three are the same. The last tiebreaker is decided by a cointoss.
