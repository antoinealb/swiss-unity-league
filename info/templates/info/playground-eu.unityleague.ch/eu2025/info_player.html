How to collect points

To participate as a player, there is no sign up needed. However to guarantee that you receive all of your points, please follow these steps:

Inform your local tournament organizer

For you to receive points, your local organizer needs to register their events for the league. Hence it's essential that you inform your local gaming store (LGS) or other independent organizers about the opportunity to participate in the league and award points.
If you organize events, sign up here:

Organizers sign up

Use your full name in tournament apps

Your full name will be the identifier used to track your points across platforms. Therefore it's really important that you use the identical full name on all tournament apps.

To pick a unique full name as your identifier, consider the following:

  • Use your first and last name with a space in between, for example "Elliot Carter".
  • Also include your middle name, for example "John Bob Smith", if your name is common in Europe.
  • If the tournament app only shows usernames, input your full name with underscores instead of spaces as the username, for example "John_Bob_Smith".

To verify that your name is correct, you can check that the standings of the app list your full name correctly.

Here are some tutorials how you can change your name in the most popular tournament apps:

Change your name in the Companion App

  1. Open the Magic: The Gathering Companion App and login
  2. Go to the Join ⚔️ tab on the bottom navigation (the one with 2 crossed swords)
  3. Click on Joining as followed by your name on top of the screen
  4. Enter your first and last name and Submit

If you need to include a middle name, just add it after your first name with a space in between. For example, add "John Bob" in the first name field.

Change your name in Melee

  1. Open Melee.gg and login
  2. Go to Manage Your Account Settings
  3. Make sure Display username by default is not checked
  4. Go to Profile
  5. Click on Edit next to your First Name and Last Name
  6. Enter your names and Save

If you need to include a middle name, just add it after your first name with a space in between. For example, add "John Bob" in the first name field.

Report inaccurate/missing results

In case the results of a tournament are incorrect or missing, it's your responsibility to report this to the organizer of the event within 14 days. If the tournament organizer is unable to correct the error, please contact us.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use a nickname or username to track my points?

To receive invites to the National Championships or the European Magic Cup, you must use your real name to track points. We will ask for ID to ensure the right players attend these events.

I'm not a national of the country where I'm playing events. Can I still participate?

Yes, you can. Your country is determined by where you play most of your events and is set automatically. If we've assigned the wrong country to you, please contact us.

Can I attend tournaments in other countries?

Yes, you collect points no matter where you play. But you only show up on the leaderboard of the country where you play most of your events.

Which countries host a National Championship and award invites to the EMC?

Generally all countries (not only EU member states) are eligible to host a National Championship and award invites to the European Magic Cup. However there must be a significant number of participating events in the country and their player base must be interested in attending the European Magic Cup.

How many players qualify in my country?

The more events/players that participate in a country, the more rewards will be handed out.
You can expect the following rewards to increase throughout the season according to the participation in a given country:

  • Number of players qualified for National Championships
  • Number of National Championship events in the country
  • EMC direct invite for the top finishers of the leadeboard

Which formats will be played?

We allow all Magic formats at our participating events, even inoffical ones. You can expect that the National Championships will be hosted in a popular format of the country.

The formats of the European Magic Cup will be chosen based on their popularity among participating events. They will be annouced throughout the first half of the year. Currently we're considering the following formats for the EMC main events:

  • Duel Commander
  • Pauper
  • Legacy
  • Modern
  • Standard
  • Limited
