League Points

Players earn league points based on their performance in each event:

  • 3 league points just for participating in an event.
  • League points equal to their total match points in the event.
  • Top finishers receive a fixed amount of points based on the event's size.

Event Types

Each Magic event gets a type, that defines its multiplier. League points awarded to players are multiplied based on the multiplier of the event.

Type Multiplier Events* European Magic Cup Invites
Local 1x FNMs, Prereleases, weeklies, leagues
Regional 3x RCQs, Store Champs, competitive, 1k
National 4x National Championships, where only the best players are invited. 4 slots
Qualifier 5x Special events in your region: Coupe de France, Nordic Masters, MOLE, etc. 1 slot
Grand Prix 6x Large Conventions: RC, Spotlight, Relic Fest, 4Seasons, etc. 1-8 slots
*We recommend to assign the given type to these events, but the assignment may be different.

National Championships

Once a player is qualified throught the leaderboard, they may attend all National Championships in their country. Each National Championships awards 4 invites to the European Magic Cup.

  • Dates: Oct - Dec 2025
  • Only the best players of the country
  • Top 4 is invited to the European Magic Cup

National Teams

Three qualified players from the same country can form a national team, each playing a different format/main event. The combined Swiss round match points count for the team result.

Prizes are awarded to the top finishers of the 3 main events and the best national teams.
