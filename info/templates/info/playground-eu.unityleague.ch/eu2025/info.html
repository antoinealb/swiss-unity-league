League Points

Players earn points based on their performance in events:

  • 3 league points just for participating
  • League points equal to match points
  • Top finishers receive a fixed amount of points based on the event's size

Event Types

Points awarded to players are multiplied based on the event type assigned by the organizer.

Type Multiplier Recommended Events
Local 1x FNMs, Prereleases, weeklies, leagues
Regional 3x RCQs, Store Champs, competitive, 1k
National 4x National Championships where best players of the country battle for 4 slots to the European Magic Cup.
Qualifier 5x Special events awarding a direct invite to the European Magic Cup.
Grand Prix 6x Conventions: RC, Spotlight, Relic Fest, 4Seasons, etc.

National Championships

Once a player is qualified throught the leaderboard, they may attend all National Championships in their country. Each National Championships awards 4 invites to the European Magic Cup.

  • Dates: Oct - Dec 2025
  • Only the best players of the country
  • Top 4 is invited to the European Magic Cup

National Teams

Three qualified players from the same country can form a national team, each playing a different format/main event. The combined Swiss round match points count for the team result.

Prizes are awarded to the top finishers of the 3 main events and the best national teams.
