Choose an event type

To each of your events, you will assign one event type. Each type has a multiplier and the higher the multiplier, the more points your players will receive.

We recommend that you assign the following types to the given events:

Type Multiplier Recommended Events EMC Invites Organizer Fees
Local 1x Weeklies, FNMs, Prereleases, leagues Free
Regional 3x Competitive 1k, RCQs, Store Champs €1 per player*
National 4x National Championships can be hosted from Oct-Dec 2025. They represent the finals of the national competition. 4 slots Free + you receive goodies
Qualifier 5x Special events in your region: Coupe de France, Nordic Masters, MOLE, etc. 1 slot Custom
Grand Prix 6x Large Conventions: RC, Spotlight, Relic Fest, 4Seasons, etc. 1-8 slot** Free**
*For Regionals with top cut the minimum fee is €25
**Only main events of European conventions with an expected attendance of 300+ players are eligible. 1 EMC slot included, additional slots can be purchased.

Organizers pay fees for some of the events they host. These fees are used to finance the goodies of the National Championships and the European Magic Cup.
You can also participate for free by running all of your events as Regular.

How to register events for the league

In the following we explain all the necessary steps to make your events award points.

First make sure to create an account, confirm your email and login.

Create an event

  1. In the Welcome menu in the top right, you click Create new event.
  2. Enter at least the Name, Date, Format, and Type of the event.
  3. On the bottom, click Create event or Schedule event as series if the event takes place regularly.
  4. To host Qualifier, National and Grand Prix events, please contact us.

Now your events will be listed in our Event calendar. To see all of your events, go to My events in the Welcome menu.

Upload results

After your event has finished, you have 30 days to upload the results.

  1. In the Welcome menu, go to Upload results.
  2. Select the tournament app* you used for the event.
  3. Select the event you want to upload results for.
  4. Login to your tournament app and download the standings from the last round of the event.
  5. Go to our import form and Submit. (for some apps you can also share a link to the standings)
  6. For most apps the top cut/playoffs is reported automatically. To do it manually click Edit playoff results.
  7. After importing the results, you can scroll down to verify that all results are imported correctly.
    8. *Alternatively you can upload the results as an Excel/CSV file or select Manual to enter them by hand.

If you have any issues, please contact us.
